University Hospitals Eye Institute

Ophthalmology Researcher Project Leader

  • Date: January 2021 - Present
  • Primary Open Angle Glaucoma is one of the leading causes of blindness in the world, yet there is not biochemical understanding of how glaucoma devleops. Our lab hopes to determine the mechanistic cause of glaucoma influential proteins like SPARC and ILK through genetic editing, immunobloting, PCR, and human eye studies.
  • My primary projects works to study the relationship between SPARC and ILK, two extracellular matrix regulator proteins. We hypothesize that SPARC regulates the ECM upstream and through ILK.
  • I am also currently investigating the role of universal ILK knockout using the gene editing system "Cre Lox". ILK is a fundamental structural protein, and knockout is normally terminal. Using this system we have managed to keep mice alive.
  • Date: September 2019 - January 2022
